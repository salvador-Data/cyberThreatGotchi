Drop-ship model

Every catalog item with a checkout button is Hacker Planet drop-ship: you pay retail price here (Stripe / PayPal / Venmo / Cash App), we order from the listed maker or distributor (supplierUrl in catalog.config.js), and it ships to your address. Typical lead time: 5–14 business days.

WiFi and wardrive tools (Pwnagotchi, Marauder, Night Hunter pods) are for authorized testing and education only. Netgotchi and CyberThreatGotchi are defensive.

Payment methods

Stripe Payment Links power card, debit, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Link checkout. PayPal Smart Buttons add PayPal and Venmo when you configure a PayPal client ID. Cash App and Venmo direct links open their apps with amount pre-filled.

Open-source software remains free on GitHub — purchases support hardware fulfillment, Pro rule curation, and Hacker Planet LLC operations.
