About us

We make defense
feel human again

Hacker Planet LLC is a Philadelphia-based security practice building open-source tools for homelabs, MSPs, and builders who refuse to choose between serious telemetry and a desk worth showing off.

Our story

Most SOCs drown in dashboards. Executives glaze over Grafana. Homelabbers wonder if their sensor is even running. We started Hacker Planet LLC to fix the signal-to-noise ratio — not by hiding complexity, but by giving it a face.

Cipherhorn, our unicorn CISO, reacts to real network events on e-ink while SQLite and hash-chain exports satisfy auditors. Same box. Two audiences. Zero bullshit about “AI-powered” when signatures would do.

We're headquartered in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania — East Coast directness, 2600 meeting energy, and a bias toward shipping STLs you can print tonight.

Principles

What we won't compromise on

🔐

Authorization first

Our tools defend and assess networks you own or are explicitly permitted to test. Full stop.

📂

Open by default

Core projects are MIT. Rule packs, STLs, and export formats ship in the repo — not a black box appliance.

📊

Evidence matters

Threat logs, CSV export, executive JSON, and tamper-evident audit chains for when legal asks questions.

✨

UX is security

If nobody watches the panel, you don't have visibility. Cipherhorn's mood is a KPI you can't ignore.

Business model

Hacker Planet LLC monetizes through hardware kits (Kickstarter / direct), Pro threat-feed subscriptions (Stripe-ready API in CyberThreatGotchi v1.1+), and MSP install retainers. See our CISO playbook for the 90-day plan and Year 1 revenue target.
