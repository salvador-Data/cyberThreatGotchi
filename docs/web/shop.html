Fulfillment, tax & authorized use

Made in Philadelphia (Sabreto Akachi, CrackBot CYD, CTG kits, custom builds) ship from Hacker Planet LLC with zone-based shipping — use the calculator above. Pennsylvania sales tax applies to PA addresses (8% in Philadelphia ZIPs: 6% state + 2% local). See Shipping & tax guide ↗.

Partner drop-ship items (Pwnagotchi, Meshtastic, Hackberry Pi, etc.) include supplier shipping in the listed price — we order from the maker after your checkout. Typical lead time: 5–14 business days.

WiFi and wardrive tools are for authorized testing and education only. Enable Stripe Tax at checkout for live compliance.

Payment methods
