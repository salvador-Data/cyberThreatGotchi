About Hacker Planet LLC

Defensive security with a human face

Hacker Planet LLC is a Philadelphia security practice - open-source edge tools, field hardware, and audit-ready telemetry for homelabs, MSPs, and builders who refuse to choose between serious defense and a desk worth showing off.

Our story

Salvador Data and Pat co-founded Hacker Planet LLC in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania to close a gap they kept seeing in the field: SOCs drowning in dashboards, executives glazing over Grafana, and homelabbers wondering if their sensor was even alive. We build defensive tools that people actually watch - with evidence auditors can trust.

Salvador Data leads product, firmware, and open-source releases (GitHub salvador-Data). Pat runs Philadelphia operations - field builds, shop fulfillment, and customer support. Together we ship from Philly, document everything on GitHub, and keep the mission squarely on authorized defense - networks and systems you own or are explicitly permitted to test.

Core projects ship under MIT. Rule packs, STLs, and export formats live in GitHub - not a black-box appliance. Every tool is for networks and systems you own or are explicitly permitted to test.
