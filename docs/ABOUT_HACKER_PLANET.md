## Who we are

**Hacker Planet LLC** builds tools that make **serious security feel approachable** — without dumbing down the telemetry. We ship open-source edge sensors, assessment companions, and pocket field kits for homelabs, MSPs, and builders who care about **authorized** defense.

Founded and operated from **Philadelphia, PA**, we blend East Coast pragmatism with Tamagotchi-grade personality: your network deserves a guardian you actually want on your desk.

## What we believe

1. **Defense is a craft** — IPS blocks and SQLite logs are not less “real” because Cipherhorn has a horn.
2. **Executives need signal, not noise** — mood and sprites translate threat pressure faster than another flat panel.
3. **Open beats opaque** — rule packs, export formats, and enclosure STLs ship with the repo.
4. **Authorization is non-negotiable** — our tools are for networks and systems you own or are explicitly permitted to test.

## Flagship product

### CyberThreatGotchi

A portable **edge IPS + Tamagotchi UI** on the Banana Pi BPI-R3 Mini. Live traffic (or simulation) feeds **Cipherhorn**, your unicorn CISO, while signatures, YARA, ClamAV, and hash deny-lists score threats. Repeat offenders get blocked; everything lands in SQLite — plus a **tamper-evident audit chain** in v1.1+.

→ [Product repo](https://github.com/salvador-Data/cyberThreatGotchi) · [Release](https://github.com/salvador-Data/cyberThreatGotchi/releases/latest) · [Web UI docs](WEB.md)
